Lore
The world of Kenshin Impact (World of Veira) is formed by a mysterious energy from a cave on earth. The cave annihilated all civilisation on earth and simultaneously created a whole new dimension. This dimension looks exactly the same as earth., However with magic powers floating around inside the world. You, as one of the first Elvas that created, started to be able to control the magic powers, and started using it for good.,

However, other Elvas that are able to control the magic power are starting to using it for malicious purposes, They use it to destroy buildings, and create dangerous and poisonous monsters. These monsters started to invade other elvas who are still in training to become the next power holder. Can you and 3 other good elvas help to save the World of Veira?, Or is the evil still going to prevail in the world?, can you become the hero?
